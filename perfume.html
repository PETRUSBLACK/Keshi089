Our Story

Crafting Timeless Fragrances

Founded in Paris in 1985, Elysium has been at the forefront of luxury perfumery for nearly four decades. Our master perfumers combine traditional techniques with innovative accords to create scents that stand the test of time.

Each bottle of Elysium contains over 120 rare ingredients sourced from around the world, including Bulgarian rose, Indian jasmine, and Madagascan vanilla. The result is a fragrance that evolves beautifully throughout the day.

We believe true luxury should be ethical. That's why we use sustainably harvested ingredients and eco-conscious packaging without compromising on quality.

Signature Notes

Top Notes

A bright opening with Italian bergamot, Sicilian lemon, and a hint of pink pepper. These fresh notes create an immediate impression that awakens the senses.

Heart Notes

The floral core features Bulgarian rose, Egyptian jasmine, and orris root. These precious flowers form the romantic heart of Elysium's composition.

Base Notes

Warm, sensual base of Madagascan vanilla, Indonesian patchouli, and rare oud. These rich notes ensure the fragrance lingers beautifully on skin.
